# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), version 2.1.

In short: be respectful and constructive. Harassment, discrimination, and
personal attacks are not tolerated. We focus on the work, assume good faith, and
keep discussion welcoming to everyone regardless of background or experience.

Unacceptable behavior can be reported to **support@voicely.art**. Reports are
handled confidentially, and the maintainers may remove comments, commits, issues,
or contributors that violate this code.
